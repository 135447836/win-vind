## Virtual Desktop  
### Switch VDesktop to Left  

|Details|Status|  
|:---|:---|  
|ID|**switch_to_left_vdesktop**|  
|N-Repeat|<span class="yes">Supported</span>|  
|Default Key|`<C-v>h`|  
|Default Command|-|  
  
It is same as **Ctrl + Win + Left** of shortcuts on Windows.  

<br>
